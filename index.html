Questions

What people ask

What is an inspired fragrance?+

An inspired fragrance is an original blend crafted to evoke the character and scent family of a well-known designer perfume. RE'NOIR blends Dubai-sourced master oils with genuine designer extract, so every bottle is built around real fragrance character rather than a flat synthetic copy.

How long does RE'NOIR fragrance last?+

RE'NOIR fragrances are concentrated oil-forward blends designed for strong presence and lasting wear. Longevity depends on skin type, weather, and the specific blend, but each bottle is tested before release.

How do I order RE'NOIR in Sri Lanka?+

RE'NOIR is available via Instagram DM at @renoir.perfume and by email at renoirperfumeceylon@gmail.com. First batch quantities are limited, so DM to check current availability.

What is the price of RE'NOIR perfume?+

All RE'NOIR fragrances are priced at Rs. 4,500 for a 10ml glass atomizer spray. This includes Noir I, Noir II, and Noir III from the debut collection.

Is RE'NOIR made in Sri Lanka?+

Yes. RE'NOIR is handcrafted in Sri Lanka using master-grade fragrance oils imported from Dubai and authentic designer extracts sourced from verified Sri Lankan retailers. Every bottle is hand-measured and hand-filled locally.
